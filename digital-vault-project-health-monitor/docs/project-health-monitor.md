# Project Health Monitor

## Overview
Project Health Monitor is a static diagnostic dashboard for active client project delivery. It helps you quickly assess operational health, identify risk, and generate clear internal/client status communication.

## Purpose
To give freelancers and studios a practical method for deciding whether a project is healthy, needs attention, is at risk, or requires immediate intervention.

## How to use during active projects
1. Fill the Project Snapshot.
2. Score all category questions (0-2).
3. Review live score, health status, weakest categories, risks, and recommended actions.
4. Copy the internal report for operations tracking.
5. Copy the client update draft and customize tone/details.
6. Print/save as PDF for internal documentation.

## Project Snapshot guide
Capture client name, project context, phase, launch target, response recency, milestone, owner, and internal notes to anchor assessment results.

## Health category explanations
- **Communication Health:** response timing, clarity, channel discipline, consolidated feedback, ownership.
- **Content & Asset Delivery:** copy/assets readiness and blockers.
- **Scope Control:** request logging, approvals, scope creep control.
- **Timeline & Milestones:** schedule realism, delay documentation, revision buffer.
- **Feedback Quality:** actionable and aligned stakeholder feedback.
- **Payment & Agreement Health:** invoice/milestone payment and term clarity.
- **Technical Delivery Health:** environment/access/integration/deployment readiness.
- **Client Decision Health:** decision-maker availability and approval flow.
- **Launch Readiness:** QA, legal/tracking/forms, post-launch readiness.

## Scoring system
- 0 = blocked/problem
- 1 = needs attention
- 2 = healthy
- 45 questions, max 90 points

## Health status interpretation
- **Critical (0-39%)**: Immediate stabilization required.
- **At Risk (40-64%)**: Serious operational risks need resolution.
- **Watch (65-84%)**: Mostly controlled, targeted corrections needed.
- **Healthy (85-100%)**: Strong delivery condition.

## Risk radar
Weak categories automatically trigger specific risk warnings:
communication delay, content blocker, scope creep, timeline, feedback chaos, payment, technical access, decision bottleneck, launch readiness.

## Recommended corrective actions
The tool proposes action steps based on weak categories/risk patterns, such as scope freeze, milestone review call, timeline updates, content checklist requests, and pre-launch QA preparation.

## Sample Project Health Report
Client: Northstar Co.
Project: Northstar Website Redesign
Phase: Development
Score: 63/90 (70%)
Status: Watch
Weakest: Content & Asset Delivery, Feedback Quality, Timeline & Milestones
Risks: Content blocker, Feedback chaos, Timeline risk
Actions: Request missing content checklist, consolidate feedback, update timeline with buffer.

## Sample Client Status Update Draft
Hi Northstar Co.,

Here is a short project status update for Northstar Website Redesign.
Current phase: Development
Overall status: Watch

Before moving to the next milestone, we should confirm remaining content, consolidate feedback, and align the updated timeline.

Recommended next steps:
1. Send missing page content.
2. Confirm feedback in one message.
3. Approve next milestone scope.

Best,

## Practical recommendations for freelancers/studios
- Run this check weekly during active production.
- Run it before milestone handoffs and before launch.
- Keep internal notes factual and action-oriented.
- Use the client draft as a neutral communication baseline.
