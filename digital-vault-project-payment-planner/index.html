How to use this planner

  1. Enter your planned project amount and choose whether VAT should be added.
  2. Select a payment model or define a custom split that totals exactly 100%.
  3. Set milestones, start date, duration, invoice prefix, and due day terms.
  4. Review the schedule, confirm totals, and copy the plan into your proposal or brief.

Recommended payment models

  • 30 / 40 / 30 — balanced for medium projects with defined delivery checkpoints.
  • 40 / 40 / 20 — stronger liquidity during build with moderate final settlement.
  • 50 / 30 / 20 — ideal when discovery and architecture are front-loaded.
  • 50 / 50 — simpler two-invoice flow for compact builds.
  • 100 upfront — useful for short engagements and quick-turn retainers.

Important billing notes

  • Confirm payment triggers in your contract for each invoice row.
  • Align milestone names with concrete deliverables or approval checkpoints.
  • Keep due-day terms consistent with your invoice templates and jurisdiction.
  • Before sending to clients, review tax setup and legal wording with professionals.
