    How to use this builder

    1. Select site type, change volume, backup strategy, and monitoring scope.
    2. Add support hours and optional service add-ons based on realistic client needs.
    3. Review the suggested tier, estimated monthly starting price, and generated summary.
    4. Copy the result and adapt it in your proposal, service catalog, or onboarding workflow.

    Common maintenance plan levels

    • Essential Care: low-complexity sites with limited monthly change.
    • Growth Care: business sites with recurring updates and light support.
    • Pro Care: dynamic websites requiring regular monitoring and technical work.
    • Priority Care: high-dependency websites needing rapid support and resilience.

    What to define before offering maintenance

    • Response time expectations and channels of communication.
    • Scope boundaries for content edits, technical tasks, and out-of-scope work.
    • Backup retention policy, restore testing expectations, and monitoring alerts.
    • Escalation process, incident windows, and billing for emergency interventions.
