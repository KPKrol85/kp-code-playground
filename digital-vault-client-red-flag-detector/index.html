Why client screening matters

Early screening reduces misalignment, protects your time, and keeps projects healthier for everyone. Clear agreements on scope, communication, and payment are often the difference between a smooth build and a preventable fire drill.

Common red flag patterns

  • Urgent timelines with unclear goals.
  • Vague scope but very specific outcomes.
  • Pressure to skip contracts, deposits, or documentation.
  • Comparison shopping that discounts expertise.
